Revolutionizing Connectivity

We develop adaptive multispectral image fusion methods optimized for object detection during all hours of the day. By fusing Red-Green-Blue (RGB), Near-Infrared (NIR) and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) sensors combined with cutting edge AI models, our solution outperform traditional single-sensor object detection methods. Furthermore, our solution allow sensor operators to automatically adjust fusion levels based on illumination and ground/air temperatures from the edge device’s attached photosensor and thermometer. This is a comparatively low-cost laser-target designator for multispectral object detection military and law enforcement applications.

Advanced Remote Sensing Solutions

We have developed Adaptive Multispectral Image Fusion methods that optimize object detection during all hours of the day. By fusing RGB and LWIR sensors, combined with state of the art AI algorithms, our models have better detection at higher confidence levels. Furthermore, the fusion level are automatically adjusted based on illumination and ground temperatures from the edge device’s attached photosensor and thermometer. The solution is adaptable to any dual-sensor ISR system, for greater interoperability between low-cost dual-sensor UAS and loitering munitions. Finally, this research develops a low-cost and open-source laser-target designator that estimates geolocation data from object classes detected by an optimized multispectral YOLO model.
