Revolutionizing Object Detection

Leveraging the expertise of our inhouse skilled engineers and scientists, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge, cost-effective AI solutions tailored for military and federal agencies. Our real-time, AI-driven object detection solutions significantly enhance target and object identification across multi-wavelength sensors on diverse platforms, including drones and satellites. With a strong foundation in multispectral object detection and geolocation from airborne platforms, we empower military and federal agencies with advanced capabilities for precise and efficient mission-critical operations

Adaptive Target identification Solutions

We develop adaptive multispectral image fusion methods optimized for object detection during all hours of the day. By fusing Red-Green-Blue (RGB), Near-Infrared (NIR) and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) sensors combined with cutting edge AI models, our solution outperform traditional single-sensor object detection methods. Furthermore, our solution allow sensor operators to automatically adjust fusion levels based on illumination and ground/air temperatures from the edge device’s attached photosensor and thermometer. This is a comparatively low-cost laser-target designator for multispectral object detection military and law enforcement applications.
